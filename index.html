Hello! We are the

Bread Basket.

We are a food pantry located in Watertown Wisconsin that strives to provide healthy and diverse fresh food to those who need it, when they need it.

Learn about how we help.

Here's a few of the things we do.

Provide Food!

We are open 3 days per week to serve our guests struggling with food insecurity. We partner with local farmers to grow in-season produce as much as our Wisconsin weather allows.

Connecting with other community resources

We partner and collaborate with other local non profits in the area guiding those with specific needs in the most appropriate direction.

Clothing

We accept donations of new and gently used clothing and distribute to our guests.
