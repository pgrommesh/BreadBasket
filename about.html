About Us

Our Mission

The Bread Basket is committed to providing dignified, choice-based access to fresh food and clothing for residents of Dodge and Jefferson counties experiencing food insecurity. We operate as a certified 501(c)(3) non-profit with no government funding, relying entirely on community support, local farmers, and generous donors.

Our model is simple: guests walk through our doors and select what they need for the week, just like a grocery store. We require no paperwork, no income guidelines, and no questions asked. Dignity, respect, and community are at the heart of everything we do.

Our History

Our Values

  • Dignity: Every person deserves to be treated with respect and provided with choice.
  • Accessibility: No paperwork, no income verification, no barriers to entry.
  • Community: We grow stronger when we work together across organizations and neighborhoods.

Our Programs

  • Choice-Based Food Pantry: Open Monday, Tuesday, and Friday. Guests select fresh and shelf-stable items tailored to their household.
  • Clothing Distribution: Accepting and distributing new and gently used clothing to guests during pantry hours.
  • Community Referrals: Collaborating with local nonprofits to guide guests with specific needs to the most appropriate resources.
