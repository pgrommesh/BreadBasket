Our Supporters & Partners

The Bread Basket is a certified 501(c)(3) non-profit that receives no government funding. Our ability to serve Dodge and Jefferson counties depends entirely on the generosity of local farmers, businesses, nonprofits, and community members. Thank you for helping us keep our doors open and our shelves stocked.

Hope Church

Hope Church has generously donated to our cause through their annual giving campaigns.

Feeding America

Feeding America is the primary organization that provides our pantry with food to serve our guests.

Round Up Program

Piggly Wiggly nominated the Bread Basket as the recipient of their "Round Up" program and raised money for us!

Shorted Date Fresh Produce & Bakery

Through our connection with Feeding America, we pick up goods 3 days per week from Costco.

Buckets for Hunger

Buckets for Hunger has provided monetary donations to our cause.

Bimbo Bakeries

Bimbo Bakeries provides our pantry with fresh bread and baked goods on a weekly basis.

Little Caesars (306 W Main St)

The local Little Caesars freezes and then donates unused pizzas on a daily basis.

Chandler House Bakery (411 E Main St)

The Chandler House Bakery has generously donated to our cause.

Want to Become a Supporter?

Businesses, farmers, nonprofits, and individuals interested in supporting The Bread Basket can contact us at breadbasketwttn@gmail.com or call (920) 390-2575.

We accept:

  • Financial donations (tax-deductible, 501(c)(3))
  • Non-perishable food items
  • New and gently used clothing
  • VOLUNTEER time for sorting, distribution, and events
