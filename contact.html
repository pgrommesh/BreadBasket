How to Get Help

If you need food or clothing assistance, simply visit us during pantry hours. No appointment is necessary, and no one will be turned away during public hours.

How to Donate

Clothing donations: We accept new and gently used clothing. Drop off during pantry hours or contact us for bulk pickup arrangements.

Financial donations: Mail a check to our address above, or contact us for online giving options. The Bread Basket is a registered 501(c)(3) organization — all financial donations are tax-deductible.
